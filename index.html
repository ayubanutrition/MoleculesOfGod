11 · Legal

Research use only.

Research use only. All compounds listed on this site are supplied exclusively for in vitro, ex-vivo, or pre-clinical research activities conducted by qualified investigators or institutions. None of these compounds are medicinal products, dietary supplements, or cosmetic ingredients. They are not approved for human consumption, administration, or therapeutic use by any regulatory authority.

No clinical claims. Any endpoint, figure, or narrative presented on this site reflects observed values in published pre-clinical literature, in-vitro assay data, or early-phase dose-finding studies. Nothing on this site constitutes a claim of efficacy, safety, or therapeutic benefit in human use.

Investigator responsibility. By purchasing any compound from Swiss Molecules, the investigator confirms that they are a qualified researcher, that all handling, storage, and experimental use will comply with the laws and regulations of their jurisdiction, and that Swiss Molecules bears no responsibility for downstream use of delivered material.

Jurisdiction. Swiss Molecules operates from Zürich, Switzerland, under Swiss federal law. Orders ship only to destinations where analytical research compounds are legal for importation under the receiving jurisdiction. Investigators are responsible for verifying local import legality before ordering.

Compounds withheld. Certain compounds are withheld from shipment to specific jurisdictions on a lot-by-lot basis. If a compound cannot ship to your jurisdiction, the order will not be placed and any authorisation will be voided without charge.
